Meet the Founder

About Dominic

The person behind Catalyst Strategy Solutions.

I spent years in the trades before I ever stepped into consulting. I've worked the jobs, dealt with the chaos, and seen firsthand what happens when companies treat their people like replaceable parts. I also saw what happened at the places that got it right—lower turnover, better work, and owners who weren't chained to their phones seven days a week.

That's what I build now.

Most companies aren't struggling because anyone stopped caring. The owners care. The people who helped build the place care. But the rock-solid leaders who held everything together got stretched too thin with no infrastructure to support them. Good workers either leave or start to shut down—not because they gave up, but because they stopped getting what they needed too.

When a company gets this right, maximum effort becomes the exception instead of the daily requirement. Problems aren't surprises—they're situations you've already planned for. You follow the plan, execute, and move on. Work stops feeling like survival and starts feeling like building something.

The owners I work with aren't looking for corporate consultants who've never swung a hammer. They need someone who gets their world and can help them fix what's broken without blowing up what's working.

If you're running a trades business and feel like you're constantly putting out fires instead of building something sustainable, I'd be glad to talk.

Let's have a conversation.

No pitch, no pressure—just a chance to talk about where you are and where you want to go.
